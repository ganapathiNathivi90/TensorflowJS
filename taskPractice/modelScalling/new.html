About

AvekSate is an IoT development platform with a latest technology stack that enables provisioning, management, and automation of connected devices with analytical capability. It helps connecting devices to the cloud and developing apps to remotely organize as well as monitor these devices with secure communications. User can build features such as dashboards, test harness, have device alerts and notifications in runtime.

Using IoT platform and solutions, help industries connect devices in an efficient, secure and reliable way as well as enabling industries to harness the wealth of data generated with analytics.

We provide powerful and flexible IoT platform for your various use cases.

Our service plans include Student, Developer and Enterprise.

We allow you to choose any of the plans and make sure that your solution is deployed faster and helps in monitoring devices remotely and securely.
